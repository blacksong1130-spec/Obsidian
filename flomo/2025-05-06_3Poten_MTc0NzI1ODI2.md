3. Potential Q&A Questions & Answers (3 minutes) 3. 可能的问答问题和答案（3分钟）

-
**Q: How does it attach to different types of office chairs? 问：它如何连接到不同类型的办公椅上？**

**A:** We've researched common office chairs, including brands like SIHOO, Okamura, and Herman Miller, and analyzed typical armrest dimensions (23-30cm long, 8-11cm wide) and shapes (mostly flat or slightly curved). We are exploring several fixation methods for universal compatibility, including top-down clamps, side clamps for unusual shapes, and flexible Velcro straps. The design considers avoiding interference with armrest adjustment buttons. **一个：** 我们研究了常见的办公椅，包括 SIHOO、Okamura 和 Herman Miller 等品牌，并分析了典型的扶手尺寸（23-30 厘米长，8-11 厘米宽）和形状（大部分是扁平的或略微弯曲的）。 我们正在探索几种通用兼容性的固定方法，包括自上而下的夹具、用于不寻常形状的侧夹和灵活的魔术贴带 。该设计考虑了避免干扰扶手调节按钮 。

-
**Q: Is the resistance level adjustable? Q： 阻力位可以调节吗？**

**A:** Yes, providing targeted, adjustable resistance is a key user requirement (63% preference). The design incorporates mechanisms, potentially like a spring-ball system, to allow users to adjust the force needed. **一个：** 是的，提供有针对性的、可调节的阻力是关键用户要求（63% 的偏好）。 该设计结合了机制，可能类似于弹簧球系统，允许用户调整所需的力。

-
**Q: What makes this different from a simple stress ball or grip strengthener? 问：这与简单的应力球或握力增强器有什么不同？**

**A:** While basic tools like grip strengtheners exist, user feedback indicates they often have limited effect. Forearm Flow is designed for dynamic, controlled motion targeting specific forearm muscles involved in common office tasks, going beyond simple squeezing. It also promotes 'dynamic rest' as advised by experts, unlike static supports. Furthermore, it integrates smart features for tracking. **一个：** 虽然存在握力增强器等基本工具，但用户反馈表明它们的效果通常有限 。Forearm Flow 专为动态、受控的运动而设计，针对常见办公室任务中涉及的特定前臂肌肉，超越了简单的挤压。与静态支撑不同，它还按照专家的建议促进“动态休息 ”。此外，它还集成了用于跟踪的智能功能。

-
**Q: What are the 'smart features'? What does it track? Q： 什么是“智能功能”？它跟踪什么？**

**A:** The design includes smart features potentially using sensors like an IMU (Inertial Measurement Unit). These can track metrics such as force applied, range of motion (angle tracking), repetitions, and duration, providing users with feedback on their exercise. This data tracking enhances user experience. **一个：** 该设计包括可能使用 IMU（惯性测量单元） 等传感器的智能功能。这些可以跟踪施加的力、运动范围 （角度跟踪）、重复次数和持续时间等指标，为用户提供有关其锻炼的反馈。此数据跟踪可增强用户体验 。

-
**Q: What is the expected price point? Q： 预期价格是多少？**

**A:** Our user research indicated a preference among 68% of potential users for a price between 50-150 yuan. While the final price isn't set, we are aiming to position it competitively, considering the value it offers over basic, low-intelligence devices and its smart capabilities. **一个：** 我们的用户研究表明，68% 的潜在用户更喜欢 50-150 元的价格。虽然最终价格尚未确定，但考虑到它相对于基本、低智能设备的价值及其智能功能 ，我们的目标是将其定位为具有竞争力。

-
**Q: What materials are used? Is it durable? Q： 使用什么材料？它耐用吗？**

**A:** We prioritized durable and comfortable materials suitable for office use. The main body might use ABS plastic, the sensor casing could be aluminum, and the ball could have a leather shell with a PU foam core for grip and comfort. Components like springs would be stainless steel, and mounting parts would use materials like Nylon. **一个：** 我们优先考虑适合办公室使用的耐用和舒适的材料。主体可能使用 ABS 塑料，传感器外壳可以是铝制的，球可以有一个带有 PU 泡沫芯的皮革外壳，以提高抓地力和舒适度 。弹簧等组件将是不锈钢，安装部件将使用尼龙等材料 。

-
**Q: Is it suitable for different hand/arm sizes? Q： 它适合不同的手/手臂尺寸吗？**

**A:** Yes, ergonomic adaptability is crucial. We've researched average hand and forearm dimensions for males and females. The design includes considerations for arm circumference compatibility and offers different ball size options (e.g., Small: 6cm, Medium: 7cm, Large: 8cm) to ensure a comfortable grip for various users. **一个：** 是的，人体工程学的适应性至关重要。我们研究了男性和女性的平均手部和前臂尺寸 。该设计包括对臂围兼容性的考虑，并提供不同的球尺寸选项（例如，小：6 厘米、中：7 厘米、大：8 厘米），以确保为不同用户提供舒适的握持。
