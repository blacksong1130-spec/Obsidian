#IRP/灵感/游戏化

夸赞一定是场景化，真正的被看见

The segment leaderboard system is one of the most effective implementations of Core Drive 5 (Social Influence & Relatedness) in any health app. When you run or ride a segment, you’re competing against every other Strava user who’s ever covered that same stretch of road. The KOM/QOM (King/Queen of the Mountain) crown system creates Core Drive 2 (Development & Accomplishment) with real geographic meaning. You didn’t just “level up.” You’re the fastest person on YOUR hill.
分段排行榜系统是任何健康应用中 Core Drive 5（社交影响力与相关性）最有效的实现之一。当你跑或骑一段路时，你是在和所有曾经走过该路段的 Strava 用户竞争。KOM/QOM（山之王/后）王冠系统创造了具有实际地理意义的核心驱动 2（开发与成就）。你并不是“升级”了。你是你那座山上最快的人。

Kudos, Strava’s version of likes, tap into Core Drive 5 differently than traditional social media. When someone gives you kudos on a rainy Tuesday morning run, it means more than a generic like because they know exactly what that effort required. The social validation is contextually rich, which makes it more motivating.
Kudos，Strava 的点赞功能，与传统社交媒体不同，它对 Core Drive 5 的使用方式不同。当有人在雨天的周二早晨跑步时给你表扬，这不仅仅是泛泛的赞赏，因为他们非常清楚那份努力需要付出什么。社会认可在情境中非常丰富，这让它更具激励性
