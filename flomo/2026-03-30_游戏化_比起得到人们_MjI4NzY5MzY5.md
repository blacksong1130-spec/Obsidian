#IRP/灵感/游戏化

比起得到，人们更加厌恶损失

Core Drive 8 (Loss & Avoidance) operates through the subscription model itself. When you’re paying $59/month, skipping a day feels like wasting money. This financial commitment creates a motivation floor that free apps lack. It’s the same principle that makes gym memberships work better than home workout videos for many people.
Core Drive 8（损失与规避）通过订阅模式本身运行。当你每月支付 59 美元时，跳过一天感觉像是在浪费钱。这种经济投入创造了免费应用所缺乏的动力底线。这也是为什么健身房会员比家庭锻炼视频对很多人来说更有效的原因。
