# 第 6 步，共 6步 形式： 您的支持文件

On the day of your appointment, please present yourself with:  
预约当天，请提交：  
  
> Your passport or any other travel document (containing at least two consecutive blank pages) + photocopy of the identity page of this document and of the last visas obtained  
> 您的护照或其他旅行证件（至少连续两页空白页）+ 该文件身份页复印件及最近获得签证的复印件  
  
> **Originals and photocopy** of all the documents listed below, translated into French or English.  
> 原件及下列所有文件的**复印**件，均可翻译成法语或英语。  

**Please note** : if any of these documents are missing, this may lead to the non-issuance of the visa you have applied for. The visa center reserves the right to ask for further documentation and information.  
**请注意** ：如果缺少任何这些文件，可能导致您申请的签证无法获得。签证中心保留索取更多文件和信息的权利。

- - **Pre-requisites   先决条件**
        
        - A travel document, issued less than 10 years ago, containing at least two blank pages, with a period of validity at least 3 months longer than the date on which you intend to leave the Schengen Area or, in the case of a long stay, at least three months longer than the expiry date of the visa requested. Be sure to transmit (scan) the identity page of your travel document as well as ALL PAGES containing visas, entry and exit stamps or any other inscription.  
            一份不到10年前签发的旅行证件，至少有两页空白页，有效期至少比你打算离开申根区的日期长3个月，或者如果是长期停留，至少比申请签证的到期日长三个月。请务必扫描（扫描）你旅行证件的身份页，以及所有包含签证、出入境章或其他任何标识的页面。
        - ID photograph.   身份证照片。
        - If you are not a British National: proof that you are legally resident in the UK : Biometric residence permit AND a printed copy of your current immigration status displaying your photo as well as a printed copy of the share code (documents printed from your UKVI account).  
            如果你不是英国公民：你需要证明你是英国合法居民：生物识别居留许可，以及一张打印出你当前移民身份的副本（上面有你的照片），还有一份由你 UKVI 账户打印的共享代码（文件）。
        - If you have an official travel document, a note verbale is required.  
            如果你有官方旅行证件，需要口头证明。
        
    - **Purpose of travel/stay   旅行/停留目的**
        
        - Proof of travel. A confirmed return ticket to France (plane, ferry, bus, etc…).  
            旅行证明。一张确认的返法国机票（飞机、渡轮、巴士等）。
        - Reservation confirmation of an organised trip or any other document describing the planned programme.  
            预订确认，或任何描述计划行程的文件。
        
    - **Socio-professional situation  
        社会职业状况**
        
        - Employee : recent signed official letter from your employer, and/or payslips for the last three months. Self-employed : Recent letter from your accountant, banker or solicitor, specifying your professional independent activity or the ownership of a company in the UK + Letter from the British financial authorities. Students : recent signed official letter from your school or University in the UK.  
            员工：最近签署的雇主正式信件和/或过去三个月的工资单。自雇人士：您的会计师、银行家或律师最近出具的信件，说明您的专业独立活动或英国公司所有权 + 英国金融当局的信函。学生：英国学校或大学最近签署的正式信件。*CF informations locales / CF local information.  
            *CF 信息地点/CF 本地信息。
        
    - **Funds   资金**
        
        - Last 3 months worth of bank statements (UK current account) showing your full name and address, and proving that you have enough funds for the whole duration of the trip, or traveller's cheques presenting the same guarantees. If you are financially sponsored by your spouse/partner : marriage certificate and bank statements of your spouse/partner.  
            过去三个月的银行对账单（英国活期账户），显示你的全名和地址，并证明你有足够的资金支付整个旅程，或者提供同样保证的旅行支票。如果你由配偶/伴侣资助：结婚证和配偶的银行对账单。
        
    - **Accommodation   住宿**
        
        - Hotel booking OR rental agreement, showing your full name as well as dates and address of stay, OR "Attestation d'accueil" (original Cerfa form, not a copy) issued by the local town hall of your host's place of residence in France, OR booking confirmation from a travel agency.  
            酒店预订合同，或提供您的全名、住宿日期和地址，或由您主人居住地在法国的地方市政厅签发的“Atestation d'accueil”（原始 Cerfa 表格，非复印件），或旅行社的预订确认。
        
    - **Travel health insurance   旅游健康保险**
        
        - Travel health insurance certificate covering all members States of the Schengen area. Minimum coverage of medical costs: at least 30 000 € (cf FAQ).  
            覆盖申根区所有成员国的旅行健康保险证书。最低医疗费用覆盖范围：至少30,000欧元（参见常见问题解答）。
