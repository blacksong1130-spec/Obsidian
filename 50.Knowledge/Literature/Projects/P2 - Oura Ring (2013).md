# P2 — Oura Ring (2013): Smart Ring for Sleep and Health

## 📖 Citation (Harvard)
Oura Health Ltd. (2013) *Oura Ring: Smart Ring for Sleep and Health* [Wearable device]. Available at: https://ouraring.com (Accessed: 6 April 2026).

---

## What / So What / Now What

**What:** Oura Ring is a wearable smart ring measuring sleep stages, HRV, skin temperature, and respiratory rate. It synthesises this complex biological data into a daily **Readiness Score** and **Sleep Score** (0–100), checked first thing in the morning. Unobtrusive design allows measurement without disrupting the behaviour being measured.

**So What:** Oura demonstrates the power of translating **invisible biology into a single emotionally meaningful number**. Sleep is as physiologically complex as nutrition, but Oura has solved the legibility problem: users understand their score, know how to affect it, and check it every morning as an emotional ritual. Dietary feedback needs a similar translation layer.

**Now What:** Oura's design principles — single daily score, unobtrusive measurement, morning emotional ritual — are a direct template for dietary feedback design. The key question: what is the nutritional equivalent of the Readiness Score, and can it be measured non-invasively?

---

## 🔑 Design Principles & Dietary Translation

| Oura Design Principle | Why It Works | Dietary Translation |
|----------------------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Single Score** | Reduces complex biology to one actionable number | "Nutrition Readiness Score" for the day |
| **Morning ritual** | Checking score = daily emotional touchpoint | "Your eating window opened well" morning notification |
| **Unobtrusive sensor** | Measures without changing the behaviour | Non-invasive wearable metabolic sensor (future) |
| **Trend over time** | Progress feels visible week-by-week | Multi-week nutritional wellbeing arc |
| **No prescription** | Score informs; doesn't command | "You could feel better with X" — not "You must eat Y" |

| Overall Assessment | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Key Strength** | Best existing model for making invisible biology emotionally legible |
| **Key Limitation** | Sleep has clearer feedback loops than nutrition; translation is non-trivial |
| **Design Lesson** | The emotional ritual of checking a score is as important as the score's accuracy |
