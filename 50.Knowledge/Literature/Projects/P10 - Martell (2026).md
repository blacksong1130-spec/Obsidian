# P10 — Martell (2026): Futures Strategy Lecture, RCA

## 📖 Citation (Harvard)
Martell, A. (2026) 'Futures Strategy: Preferred Futures and Scenario Building'. Lecture delivered at Royal College of Art, London, March 2026.

---

## What / So What / Now What

**What:** Andreis Martell's guest lecture presented the Connected Places Catapult's approach to futures strategy, organising futures practice around four stages: **Intelligence** (gathering signals), **Perspective** (identifying implications), **Logic** (building scenarios), and **Foresight** (imagining preferred futures). Also introduced backcasting, preferred futures, and the Futures Cone as practical tools.

**So What:** This lecture provided the futures methodology framework for this project. The **Intelligence–Perspective–Logic–Foresight (IPLF)** model structures the research and design methodology in Section 5: gathering signals about metabolic technology and food culture (I), identifying design implications (P), building 2035 scenarios (L), and working backwards from a preferred future via backcasting (F).

**Now What:** Apply the full IPLF framework as a checkpoint structure for the project plan. The backcasting technique in particular will identify specific design decisions needed in 2026–2027 to reach the preferred 2035 future. This is the primary futures methodology framework — not just a lecture reference.

## 🔑 IPLF Framework Applied to This Project

| IPLF Stage | General Meaning | This Project's Application |
|-----------|----------------|---------------------------|
| **Intelligence** | Gather signals about the present and emerging futures | CGM development · anti-diet shift · GLP-1 drugs · AI nutrition tools |
| **Perspective** | Identify what the signals imply | Metabolic feedback is coming; ethical design framework is urgently needed |
| **Logic** | Build scenario frameworks | Three 2035 scenarios: High-Tech Biometric · Ambient Social · Intuitive Technology |
| **Foresight** | Imagine preferred futures; backcast to present | Preferred: eating well feels meaningful without anxiety → work backwards to now |

| Backcasting Question | Answer |
