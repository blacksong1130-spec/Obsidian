#IRP/研究过程/prototype

好，按照信里的五个问题：

**I am working on a research project concerned with...**

Why eating well feels like nothing — why there is no moment of "I did well today" in dietary behaviour, even when a person is genuinely trying.

为什么好好吃饭没有任何感觉——为什么即使一个人真的在努力，也没有"我今天做得很棒"的时刻。

**The thing I most need to understand — and cannot get from reading or conversation alone — is...**

How a person actually responds when they are shown a simple, immediate signal about their dietary effort. Does it feel motivating or intrusive? Does it create the feeling of "I did well today" — or does it feel like surveillance?

当一个人看到关于自己饮食努力的简单即时信号时，他们的真实反应是什么。这种感觉是激励还是侵入？是"我今天做得很棒"——还是被监视的感觉？

**I need you to make this visible / felt / debatable / possible to encounter. Specifically, I need you to...**

Place a simple feedback moment in front of someone after they eat — something that says "today you did well" or "today was a trade-off" — and let me watch what happens.

在某人吃完饭后，给他们一个简单的反馈时刻——告诉他们"今天你做得很好"或"今天是一个权衡"——然后让我观察会发生什么。

**The people who encounter you should feel / think / notice / question...**

Whether their daily food choices have meaning. Whether effort without visible reward is worth making. Whether knowing more about what they eat makes them feel empowered or anxious.

他们的日常饮食选择是否有意义。没有可见回报的努力是否值得坚持。了解更多关于自己饮食的信息，是让他们感到有力量还是更有负担。

**You will have done your job when I know something I did not know before you existed — specifically...**

Whether meaningful dietary feedback makes people feel more in control of their health, or more burdened by it.

有意义的饮食反馈是让人们感到对自己的健康更有掌控感，还是更有负担。

**Yours in advance,**

Weijie Li / Why does eating well feel like nothing — and can design change that?

李威杰 / 为什么好好吃饭没有任何感觉——设计能改变这一点吗？
