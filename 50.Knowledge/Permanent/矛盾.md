#IRP/灵感/核心/矛盾

**Most surprising tension I found:**

Technology supports individual tracking（科技支持个人追踪），but Nature operates collectively（自然以集体方式运作）。你的肠道菌群是几万亿个生命体共同决定你对食物的反应，但所有健康科技都把你当成一个独立个体来追踪。 Technology treats the body as an individual system, but nature operates as a collective ecosystem — your gut microbiome is trillions of organisms making decisions together.

**What this tension reveals about my problem:**

饮食反馈之所以难以设计，是因为身体对食物的反应是集体的、延迟的、非线性的——这和科技追踪的逻辑（个体的、即时的、线性的）根本上是矛盾的。这不是技术问题，是范式问题。 Dietary feedback is hard to design because the body's response to food is collective, delayed, and non-linear — fundamentally incompatible with how technology tracks things.
