#IRP/灵感/游戏化

把抽象思维变成具象的物品

Noom’s color-coding system (green, yellow, red foods) is a masterclass in Core Drive 3 (Empowerment of Creativity & Feedback). Instead of counting calories with mathematical precision, users learn to make visual assessments of their plates. This simplification transforms the overwhelming complexity of nutrition science into an intuitive framework that people can actually use in real-time at a restaurant.
Noom 的颜色编码系统（绿色、黄色、红色食物）是核心驱动 3（创造力与反馈赋能）的典范。用户不再用数学精确计算卡路里，而是学会对盘子进行视觉评估。这种简化将营养科学的复杂性转化为一个直观的框架，人们可以在餐厅实时使用。
