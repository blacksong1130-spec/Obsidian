#IRP/灵感/游戏化

多维的评价体系

The relative effort feature adds Core Drive 7 (Unpredictability). Even if your pace was slower today, your heart rate data might reveal that your effort was actually higher than yesterday’s “faster” run. This reframing turns disappointing workouts into meaningful data points, reducing the discouragement that makes people quit other tracking systems.
相对努力功能增加了 Core Drive 7（不可预测性）。即使你今天配速变慢，心率数据也可能显示你的努力实际上比昨天“更快”的跑位更高。这种重新定义将令人失望的锻炼转化为有意义的数据点，减少了人们放弃其他追踪系统的气息。
