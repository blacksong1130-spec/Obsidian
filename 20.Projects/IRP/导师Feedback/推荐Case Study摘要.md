### Context 背景
Karl Krukow is a leading behaviour designer (guest lecturer on the tutor's behaviour design course) with a portfolio of food-related and sustainability-related behavioural design projects. The specific case study covers a collaboration with **COOP** (major European grocery retailer) to redesign in-store choice environments for climate-friendly purchasing.

Karl Krukow是行为设计领域的领军人物（曾在导师的行为设计课程中担任客座讲师），拥有一系列与食物及可持续性相关的行为设计项目组合。该具体案例研究涵盖与**COOP**（欧洲主要连锁超市）的合作，旨在重新设计店内选择环境，以促进气候友好型购买行为。

### Design Approach 设计方法
- **Behavioural Design + Nudge Theory:** Redesigning environments so that the sustainable/healthy choice becomes the *intuitive default* — no conscious effort required.
  **行为设计 + 助推理论：** 重新设计环境，使可持续/健康选择成为*直觉性默认选项*——无需有意识的努力。
- **Three core interventions:**
  **三个核心干预措施：**
  1. **Point-of-Sale (POS) redesign** — visual highlighting of climate-friendly options at decision moments
     **销售点（POS）重设计** — 在决策时刻视觉突出气候友好选项
  2. **Space management** — strategic product placement prioritising sustainable items
     **空间管理** — 战略性产品摆放，优先展示可持续商品
  3. **In-store nudges** — environmental cues encouraging eco-conscious selections
     **店内助推** — 鼓励环保选择的环境暗示

### Outcomes 成果
| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| CO₂ reduction | **14% in 6 months** (exceeding 2030 targets by 5 years) |
| Sales of climate-friendly products | **+25%** |
| Net turnover growth (test locations) | **+123%** |
| Food waste reduction | **-60%** |
| Customer perception (climate-conscious) | **+65%** |

### Key Insight for IRP 对IRP的关键启示
> The study demonstrates that ==**sustainability and profitability operate synergistically**,== not competitively. Embedding behavioural insights into design achieves measurable behavioural change.

> 该研究表明，**可持续性与盈利性是协同运作的**，而非竞争关系。将行为洞察嵌入设计可实现可量化的行为改变。

**Relevance to IRP / 与IRP的关联：**
- Demonstrates how ==**environmental design**== (not individual willpower) can shift food choices
  展示了**环境设计**（而非个人意志力）如何改变食物选择
- The B=MAP model in action: reducing *ability cost* and strengthening *prompt* at point of decision
  Fogg B=MAP模型的实践：在决策时刻降低*能力成本*并强化*提示*
- Key question for IRP: Can similar nudge principles be applied to *feedback design* for individual dietary behaviour?
  IRP的关键问题：类似的助推原则能否应用于个人饮食行为的*反馈设计*？

### Research Methods Used 使用的研究方法
- **Primary:** Surveys and interviews (across different cooking skill levels)
  **初级研究：** 调查与访谈（涵盖不同烹饪技能水平）
- **Direct observation:** Studying kitchen environments and food interaction
  **直接观察：** 研究厨房环境与食物互动
- **Hands-on experimentation:** Building a hydroponic farm (prototyping + learning)
  **实践实验：** 建造水培农场（原型制作+学习）
- **Case study analysis:** Examining Cloughjordan village (Ireland's lowest carbon footprint community)
  **案例分析：** 研究Cloughjordan村庄（爱尔兰碳足迹最低社区）
- **Secondary:** Existing food waste solutions and natural preservation methods
  **二级研究：** 现有食物浪费解决方案和天然保存方法

### Key Findings 关键发现
- "Convenience, lack of awareness, and inadequate food storage practices" = primary causes of food waste
  "便利性、缺乏意识和不充分的食物储存方式"= 食物浪费的主要原因
- ~931 million tonnes food wasted globally/year; households responsible for **61%**
  全球每年约9.31亿吨食物被浪费；家庭占**61%**

### {>>所以呢
<<}Design Output — "The Living Kitchen" 设计输出——"活体厨房"
An integrated "living kitchen" concept combining:
一个综合性"活体厨房"概念，整合：

- Window-mounted **hydroponic systems** for fresh herbs/vegetables
  窗挂**水培系统**（新鲜香草/蔬菜）
- **Seaweed farms** using greywater
  利用灰水的**海藻农场**
- Antibacterial shelving for temperature regulation
  抗菌货架（温度调节）
 ==Transparent refrigeration for visibility==
  ==透明冰箱（食材可视化）==
- Systems converting **food scraps into useful resources**
  将**食物残渣转化为有用资源**的系统



### Relevance to IRP / 与IRP的关联
- Use for **methods inspiration**: how to combine primary research, observation, and speculative prototyping
  用于**方法灵感**：如何结合初级研究、观察与思辨原型设计
- Reconnecting consumers with food production — resonates with IRP's embodiment framing
  重新连接消费者与食物生产——与IRP的具身框架产生共鸣

### Key Technologies & Trends 关键技术与趋势

| Technology | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Precision Fermentation 精确发酵** | Creating ingredients at scale, including hard-to-produce ones; healthier fats and oils |
| **Smart Packaging 智能包装** | Connected packaging providing real-time, science-backed consumer insights |
| **Functional Beverages 功能饮料** | Nano-bubbles, encapsulations targeting gut health and cognition |
| **GLP-1 Medication Integration** | Food products designed to work with weight-loss medications |

### Major Trends 主要趋势
1. **Clean-label demand** — reducing additives while maintaining quality
   **清洁标签需求** — 减少添加剂同时保持品质
2. **Ultra-Processed Food (UPF) debate** — evidence-based reformulation vs broad classification
   **超加工食品（UPF）争论** — 循证重新配方vs广义分类
3. **Traditional ingredient revival** — e.g., carob for modern wellness
   **传统成分复兴** — 如角豆用于现代健康

### Key Quote 关键引言
> *"The convergence of science, technology, and consumer values defines the future landscape."*
> *"科学、技术与消费者价值观的融合定义了未来格局。"*

### Relevance to IRP / 与IRP的关联
- Represents **current technology landscape signals** for the IRP's Quantified Self 2.0 trend
  代表IRP"量化自我2.0"趋势的**当前技术格局信号**
- Smart packaging = alternative **ambient feedback mechanism** (non-app-based dietary feedback)
  智能包装 = 替代性**环境反馈机制**（非应用程序的饮食反馈）
- The gap: even cutting-edge food tech focuses on **product innovation**, not **feedback design experience**
  差距：即使是前沿食品科技也聚焦于**产品创新**，而非**反馈设计体验**

## 💡 Synthesis for IRP 对IRP的综合启示

1. **Behavioural design (Krukow)** shows that *environmental design at decision points* is more powerful than individual willpower — this reinforces the IRP's focus on feedback system design over motivation.
   **行为设计（Krukow）**表明，*决策时刻的环境设计*比个人意志力更强大——这强化了IRP对反馈系统设计而非动机的聚焦。

2. **Speculative futures (Stanford)** provides methodological precedent for applying design futures methods to the food-body-technology nexus.
   **思辨未来（斯坦福）**为将设计未来方法应用于食物-身体-技术关系提供了方法论先例。

3. **Mixed methods (Future of Food 2050)** demonstrates how primary research + hands-on prototyping + case analysis can work together in a design futures project.
   **混合方法（2050年食物的未来）**展示了初级研究+实践原型制作+案例分析如何在设计未来项目中协同运作。

4. **Technology signals (Future Food Tech)** maps the current innovation landscape — useful for grounding speculative scenarios in real technological trajectories.
   **技术信号（未来食品科技）**绘制了当前创新格局——有助于将思辨情景根植于真实的技术轨迹。
