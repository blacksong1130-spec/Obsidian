# 🗂️ Problem Framing Cards

> [!abstract] The core challenges addressed by this project.

## 🃏 Card 1: The Intention-Action Gap
**The "Phenomenological Silence" of Nutrition**
> "Why does eating well feel like nothing?"
- **The Gap:** We fail to sustain healthy eating not due to lack of motivation, but because good nutrition produces **no immediate, perceivable signal of success**. 
- **The Consequence:** Without feedback, healthy behaviour cannot be reinforced, iterated, or emotionally validated.

## 🃏 Card 2: Hyperbolic Discounting in Diet
**Present Bias vs. Future Health**
> "The immediate pleasure of eating badly always defeats the abstract promise of future health."
- **System 1 Thinking:** Dietary decisions are made hundreds of times daily in System 1 (fast, automatic). 
- **The Design Failure:** Current tools like MyFitnessPal operate in System 2 (requiring effortful manual logging), leading to a **30-day retention rate of only 3%**.

## 🃏 Card 3: The Eating Disorder Ethical Constraint
**When Tracking Becomes Harmful**
> "Intensive self-monitoring correlates with elevated disordered eating risk."
- **The Risk:** Hospital admissions for eating disorders in the UK increased by **84%** between 2015 and 2021.
- **The Ethical Mandate:** Any future feedback system must avoid surveillance and anxiety, instead actively supporting **Food Freedom** and a healthy body-food relationship.
